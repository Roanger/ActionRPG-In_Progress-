# Loot & Equipment System Design

## Theme: "Mechanized Evolution"
Players do not just wear armor; they replace and upgrade their robot's physical components.

## Equipment Slots (9 Total)
| RPG Standard | Robot Theme | Description | Primary Stat |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Helm** | **Cranial Unit** | The head sensor array and processing housing. | Detection / Crit Chance |
| **Chest** | **Chassis Core** | The main body shell protecting the reactor. | Structure (HP) / Armor |
| **Bracers** | **Arm Actuators** | Hydraulic pumps and servo guards for arms. | Melee Dmg / Stability |
| **Pants** | **Locomotion Struts**| The upper leg pistons and joints. | Carry Weight / HP |
| **Boots** | **Treads / Thrusters**| The feet or wheels contacting the ground. | Velocity (Speed) |
| **Ring (x2)** | **Logic Chip** | Small processing units inserted into slots. | Cooldown / Special Effects |
| **Belt** | **Power Regulator** | Manages energy flow from the core. | Energy Regen / Shield |
| **Necklace** | **Data Uplink** | A suspended module or antenna. | Tech/Elemental Dmg |
| **Weapon** | **Armament** | Integrated or handheld weapons. | Output (Damage) |

## Stat Mapping
- **Structure**: Max Health
- **Output**: Base Damage
- **Torque**: Physical Damage Bonus
- **Voltage**: Energy/Elemental Damage Bonus
- **Cycle Rate**: Attack Speed
- **Cooling**: Cooldown Reduction
- **Velocity**: Movement Speed

## Item Rarity (Quality)
1.  **Scrap (White)**: Rusted, barely functional parts.
2.  **Refurbished (Green)**: Cleaned and standard operating condition.
3.  **Tempered (Blue)**: Reinforced and optimized.
4.  **Overclocked (Purple)**: Running beyond factory limits.
5.  **Sentient (Orange)**: Parts that seemingly have a mind of their own.

## Class Weaponry
- **Juggernaut**: *Impact Hammer*, *Power Fist*, *Pile Bunker*.
- **Mage (Arc-Bot)**: *Tesla Coil*, *Plasma Vent*, *Arc Caster*.
- **Ranger (Ballistic-Bot)**: *Autocannon*, *Grenade Launcher*, *Railgun*.
